Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

1. Introduction

Genius Trainer ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Profile data: age, gender, height, weight, fitness goals, and activity level
  • Workout and meal completion data
  • Language preferences

With your explicit permission, we may also access:

  • Apple HealthKit data (steps, heart rate, sleep, weight) — only if you grant access

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate personalized AI fitness and nutrition plans
  • To track your progress over time
  • To improve app performance and user experience

4. Data Storage

Your profile and workout data are stored locally on your device using SwiftData. We do not maintain a backend database with your personal information.

AI-generated plans may be processed through third-party AI APIs (such as OpenAI or Groq). Requests sent to these services contain only fitness-related inputs and no personally identifiable information.

5. HealthKit Data

If you grant permission, Genius Trainer reads health metrics from Apple HealthKit solely to improve your coaching experience. We do not sell, share, or use HealthKit data for advertising purposes. HealthKit data is never sent to third-party analytics services.

6. Third-Party Services

The app may use the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI / Groq – AI plan generation
  • RevenueCat – Subscription management
  • Google AdMob – Advertising (free tier users)

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing their data practices.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Since data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app. There is no server-side data to delete.

8. Children's Privacy

Genius Trainer is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
